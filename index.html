Biography

Nightwing, also known as Richard "Dick" Grayson, is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. He is the first character to assume the role of Batman's crime-fighting partner, Robin, and is the leader of the teen titans. Dick Grayson was an acrobat at a traveling circus, where he performed with his parents and siblings, known as the "Flying Graysons". Tragedy struck when the circus was targeted by the notorious crime lord Tony Zucco, who had the Graysons killed in order to silence their testimony against him.

Witnessing the murder of his family, a young Dick Grayson was taken in by Bruce Wayne, who trained him to become the first Robin.As Robin, Grayson fought alongside Batman in Gotham city, taking down notorious criminals such as the Joker and the Riddler. However, as he grew older and more skilled, he began to chafe under Batman's strict rules and regulations. Eventually, he decided to strike out on his own and adopted the identity of Nightwing. As Nightwing, Grayson continued to fight crime in Gotham City and later in Bludhaven.

He is a skilled acrobat and hand-to-hand combatant, trained by Batman, as well as a skilled detective and leader. He has a long-standing rivalry with the villain Deathstroke and has been a member of the Teen Titans and the Justice League. He has appeared in multiple comics series, animated series, video games, and live-action films. Throughout his career as Nightwing, Grayson has struggled with his identity and his relationship with Batman. He has gone through several different costumes and even changed his name, but always remained a prominent and respected member of the DC Universe, as well as a mentor to many young heroes.

Fast Facts

  • - He took the name "Nightwing" from a legendary Kryptonian hero, who was actually a non-powered Superman fighting crime in the bottled city of Kandor.

  • - Out of everyone in the Batman family, the Joker hates Nightwing the most.

  • - Nightwing's suit is insulated against extreme temperatures, electric shocks and protects against projectile weapons.

  • - Nightwing once decided to join the Bludhaven police department in an attempt to get rid of the corruption of the city from within.

  • - Nightwing and Starfire actually have a daughter together named Nightstar. Nightstar can fly, absorb and protect energy and even has enhanced strength.
